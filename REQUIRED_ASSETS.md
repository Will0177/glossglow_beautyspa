## Image Guidelines

### General Requirements:
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Quality:** High resolution, professional photography
- **Style:** Consistent lighting and color tone across all images
- **Content:** Professional, elegant, representing beauty and wellness
- **Optimization:** Images should be optimized for web (compressed but high quality)

### Color Tone:
- Warm, inviting, elegant
- Soft lighting
- Neutral backgrounds with pops of color
- Professional and clean aesthetic

### Content Guidelines:
- All images should reflect the premium, professional nature of the business
- Avoid overly staged or stock-photo-looking images
- Focus on natural beauty and authentic moments
- Ensure diversity and inclusivity in any client photos
- Maintain privacy and professionalism in all images
