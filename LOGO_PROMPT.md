# Logo Design Prompt for Glow Beauty Salon & Spa

## Logo Design Brief

**Business Name:** Glow Beauty Salon & Spa

**Design Style:**
- Modern and elegant
- Sophisticated yet approachable
- Reflects luxury and wellness
- Clean, minimalist aesthetic

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: #BCAAA4 (Soft taupe/beige - warm, calming)
- Secondary: #5C4033 (Deep brown - elegant, grounding)
- Accent: #FDFCF8 (Cream/off-white - clean, fresh)
- Optional: Soft gold or rose gold accents

**Design Elements:**
- Incorporate a subtle glow or radiance effect
- Consider elegant typography (serif or modern sans-serif)
- May include a subtle icon or symbol related to:
  - Beauty/radiance (sparkle, glow, light)
  - Wellness (leaf, water droplet, circle)
  - Elegance (crown, star, abstract shape)
- Avoid overly decorative or busy designs

**Typography:**
- Font family: Cormorant Garamond (serif) or similar elegant serif
- Alternative: Modern, clean sans-serif for a contemporary look
- Letter spacing: Wide, elegant tracking
- Style: Uppercase or title case

**Logo Variations Needed:**
1. Full logo (text + icon)
2. Icon only (for favicon and small applications)
3. Horizontal layout
4. Vertical/stacked layout
5. Monochrome version (for single-color printing)

**Usage:**
- Website header
- Business cards
- Social media profiles
- Marketing materials
- Signage

**Mood/Feeling:**
- Luxurious
- Calming
- Professional
- Welcoming
- Refined

**Avoid:**
- Clich√© beauty symbols (lipstick, mirror, etc.)
- Overly feminine or masculine designs
- Complex illustrations
- Bright, neon colors

**Final Deliverables:**
- High-resolution PNG (transparent background)
- SVG format (vector)
- Favicon (16x16, 32x32, 48x48)
- Color and monochrome versions
